About Kushan

Hi, My name is Kushan Patel and I am a Web Developer. After going to school to learn Computer Science, I decided it was time to pursue a new challenge, and a career change to front end development was made. In February I became a Spring/Summer cohort at Thinkful. I build web sites that are easily usable from a wide array of devices without requiring separate sites for different platforms. I now spend my days writing HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript/jQuery with future plans to learn Node.js. Currently I reside in New Jersey.

My hobbies include sports, hiking, writing comedy, learning French, traveling. I'm also known to be a die hard New York Giants, Knicks, Yankees, Rangers fan.
